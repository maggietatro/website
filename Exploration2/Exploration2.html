Bio

I'm methodical.

I like to be prepared. I make lists, I schedule things, I meet deadlines. I have a process-driven and solution-oriented approach to project management. I do my research and, as much as possible, I try to anticipate potential setbacks before collaborating with and presenting to developers, stakeholders, and other design professionals.

I like to be challenged.

It's important to hear positive feedback, but it's equally important to hear when something isn't working. If a user struggles to interact with or understand a design, I work hard to make improvements to the usability, functionality, and presentation of the design. When I get stuck, I brainstorm with my co-workers, re-read passages of design books, or look for inspiration on the internet.

I value work-life balance.

I love what I do professionally, but I also make time for non-professional interests. I enjoy reading, traveling, music, comedy, hanging out with my husband, staying in touch with family, trying new restaurants, and playing board games with friends.
