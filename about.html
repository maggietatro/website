Maggie Tatro

Hi! I'm Maggie.

I'm a UX Designer at MSU Federal Credit Union and consider myself lucky to be surrounded by a team of super helpful and skilled IT professionals.

Professionally, I'm drawn to work that requires attention to detail and a combination of independence and collaboration. I began working in middle school and over the years I've accumulated weird, wacky, and wonderful employment experiences that have been hugely educational - sometimes I refer back to those experiences to inform my decisions as a designer.

Personality-wise, I'm ambiverted and comfortable interacting with different people. I don't shy away from opportunities to improve my communication skills and I try to interact with others in ways that make them feel at ease. I prefer to observe and listen before I speak, to maintain a positive attitude, and to treat challenges as learning opportunities.

My hobbies include reading, traveling, hiking, singing along to my favorite songs, and hosting board game nights with my husband, Kevin.
