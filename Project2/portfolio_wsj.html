Maggie Tatro

The Wall Street Journal

I was hired by Saideh Ghods, founder of Mahak, to photograph a headshot of her that was published in The Wall Street Journal’s The 50 Women to Watch 2008.

This freelance opportunity introduced me to an inspirational person and to the intricacies of freelance work.

I spent several years as a part-time freelance photographer and managed multiple projects in the US and abroad. I worked with diverse clients to determine and succesfully meet project specifications and deadlines.

Eventually, I sold my photography equipment and stopped freelancing so I could focus on developing skills that aligned with my long-term professional goals.

I still have a deep appreciation for photography - but I no longer enjoy being behind the camera.

Tools: Nikon D40, Velbon tripod, Adobe Creative Suite
