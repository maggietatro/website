Michigan State University Federal Credit Union

Michigan State University Federal Credit Union (MSUFCU) is the largest university-based credit union in the world in membership and asset size and is the official financial services partner of Oakland University.

As a UX Designer at MSUFCU, I work on projects that impact employees, members, and the public. A lot of the work I do is proprietary information, so I'm unable to provide details of all of my work on a public site like this.

Tools: Sketch, UXPin, JIRA, Confluence, Adobe Creative Suite, Getty Images

Web Design

I went through several iterations before deciding on this final site design for my portfolio site. I did a lot of research and created a series of wireframes before I started coding.

I was exposed to a lot of new technical information in a short amount of time and used this knowledge to develop this site from scratch.

There were a few times that I considered abandoning this idea for something less challenging, but resources on the internet, coding gurus around me, and my own stick-to-itiveness got me through.

There is a lot I haven't tackled yet so I'm continuing to expand my web design skills by experimenting, exploring, and asking lots of questions.

Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Wireframe.cc, Github, Atom, W3Schools, Google Dev Tools
