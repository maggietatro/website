STYLE GUIDE

experiment assignment due tuesday of week fifteen

We are working toward the final project. As a preliminary assignment, you will create a style guide webpage to explore your aesthetics and build your components.

This is a preliminary exploration assignment to work through the elements you will need. The idea is to separate code and content during the early stages of the project. This should be an active style guide that will probably change as you develop your project.

While not required, you may want to collect examples of existing websites to emulate, mood/inspiration boards, code snippets, etc. These will help you to figure out how your project will look and how it will function.

Required components/modules to include:

  • the project story: a short explainer to put the project in context
  • structure (layout blocks, responsive, media queries)
  • navigation
  • show typography (h1, h2, h3, p)
  • show link and hover effects
  • lists
  • colors
  • any other modules/components you need

Additional components/modules you may need:

  • header and footer
  • image gallery
  • blockquotes
  • anything else??
