Additional packages that I use...

  • Atom Beautify - cleans up your whitespace, adds indents, etc. for better looking code.
  • Autoclose HTML - automatically closes tags when you open them. I like this one a lot.
  • Simple Drag Drop Text - highlight text and drag it somewhere else in the code. I really, really, really like this one.
  • File Icons - adds icons & formatting to your files tree list.
  • Zen - distraction free writing for when you want to hide the interface.
